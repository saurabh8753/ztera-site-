How to Watch TeraBox Videos on PC or Laptop

You don’t need any app to watch TeraBox videos on PC. Just use ZTERA Web Player.

Steps

  1. Copy TeraBox link from phone
  2. Open PC browser
  3. Go to ZTERA Player
  4. Paste link → Play
